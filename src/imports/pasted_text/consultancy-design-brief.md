Design a premium, conversion-focused website for a UK Environmental, Energy, Sustainability, Fire Safety, and Building Compliance Consultancy.

The company provides specialist consultancy services including SAP Calculations, SBEM Modelling, EPCs, Part L Compliance, BREEAM Assessments, Fire Risk Assessments, Fire Strategy Reports, Flood Risk Assessments, Biodiversity Net Gain (BNG), Acoustic Assessments, Building Regulations Consultancy, Airtightness Testing, Asbestos Surveys, and related compliance services.

Target clients are property developers, architects, planning consultants, contractors, investors, housing associations, commercial asset owners, and construction professionals across the United Kingdom.

The visual style must feel comparable to premium engineering and multidisciplinary consultancies rather than technology startups. The website should communicate technical expertise, regulatory authority, trust, speed, and commercial competence.

Avoid generic sustainability clichés such as leaves, recycling symbols, cartoon eco graphics, wind turbines floating in white space, bright environmental branding, or stock imagery showing people planting trees.

Instead use:

• Architectural drawings
• Technical plans
• Building cross-sections
• Modern developments
• Construction details
• Engineering diagrams
• Compliance documentation visuals
• Building performance graphics
• Clean grid systems
• Structured layouts
• Professional photography of commercial and residential developments

The website should feel capable of supporting everything from individual residential compliance assessments through to major multi-disciplinary development projects requiring environmental, sustainability, fire, and regulatory consultancy.

Design inspiration:

• Arup
• WSP
• Ramboll
• Sweco
• AECOM
• Buro Happold

The overall impression should be:

• Authoritative
• Technical
• Fast
• Reliable
• Commercial
• Professional
• Established
• Trusted

IMPORTANT:

Do not display pricing, rates, fees, packages, budgets, quotations, or cost estimates anywhere within the design.

Do not create pricing tables.

Do not include "starting from" pricing.

The website should position the company as a professional consultancy partner rather than a commodity service provider.
